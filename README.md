# 🪓 Timber Harvest | Woodworker Simulator

**Version:** v1.1.2 - Restructuring locations

**Developer:** Mx. Olly

**Platform:** Web Browser (HTML5, JavaScript, Three.js)

**Genre:** First-Person Simulation / Resource Management

---

## 🌲 About the Game

**Timber Harvest** is an immersive, first-person web game where you build and manage your own lumber empire from the ground up. Placed in a dynamic 3D environment, players must manage their time and resources wisely to turn an empty plot of land into a thriving business.

The game features a tactile, grid-based drag-and-drop inventory system, tool upgrades, and a seamless day/night economy loop. What begins as a simple loop of chopping trees and selling logs quickly evolves into a complex management game involving processing materials, base building, and customer simulation.

---

## 🎯 The Goals

Your primary objective is to build the ultimate lumber business. To achieve this, you must:

* **Harvest Resources:** Head into the designated forest zone and chop down trees.
* **Manage Inventory:** Use the terminal UI to drag, drop, and organize your hotbar and backpack efficiently.
* **Upgrade Gear:** Reinvest your profits into stronger axes and larger backpacks to increase your yield.
* **Expand & Build:** Process raw logs into planks to construct crucial infrastructure, including a storage shed, a storefront, and a permanent upgrade board.
* **Automate Sales:** Keep your store stocked so customers can purchase goods while you are busy expanding the operation.

## 🗺️ Development Roadmap & ETAs

The game is actively in development. Below is the planned trajectory for upcoming features:

### Phase 1: The Core Loop (Completed ✅)

* First-person camera controls and movement boundaries.
* Instanced mesh forest generation with localized tree health.
* Raycast-based chopping and physical item dropping.
* Drag-and-drop inventory UI with hotbar persistence.
* Day/Night clock and basic economy system.

### Phase 2: Processing & Crafting (ETA: Mid-June 2026)

* **Universal Loading Manager:** Implementation of a master loading screen to handle multiple 3D assets without crashing.
* **The Wood Saw:** A new interactive workstation to process raw logs into usable wood planks.
* **Zoning:** Strict division of the map into a safe "Store Zone" for building and a "Forest Zone" for harvesting.

### Phase 3: Base Building (ETA: Late-June 2026)

* **Blueprint System:** Placeable holograms that players can feed with planks to construct buildings.
* **Infrastructure:** Construction of the core base (Storage Shed, Store Shack, Upgrade Sign).
* **Collision Detection:** Ensuring players cannot walk through newly constructed buildings.

### Phase 4: The Customer Update (ETA: July 2026)

* **Customer AI:** Basic NPC pathfinding to simulate customers walking up to the storefront.
* **Dynamic Pricing:** Supply and demand mechanics based on store stock and pricing terminals.
